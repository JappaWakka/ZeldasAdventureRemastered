To enable Debug Mode, name your savefile MVBARICKMAN.
You will not be able to die. Your hearts will simply stay empty.
You can also use these cheats:

Keypad 0 - Fill HP to max
Keypad 1 - Decrease current HP by half a heart
Keypad 2 - Increase current HP by half a heart
Keypad 3 - Decrease max HP by a whole heart
Keypad 4 - Increase max HP by a whole heart
Keypad 5 - Add 250 Rubies
Keypad 6 - Adds a single item to the inventory (originally used when I hadn't implemented NPCs yet, currently adds an Empty Pitcher)
Keypad 7 - Adds all available treasures and weapons to the inventory (many have not been implemented yet and only subtract from your Rubies)
Keypad 8 - Double player speed (press again to reset to original speed)
Keypad 9 - Changes the currently visible map in the World Map screen (Overworld or one of the Shrines)
Keypad / - Marks every tile (both Overworld and Shrines) as visited on the World Map screen
